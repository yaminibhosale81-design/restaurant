This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of guests who use our hotel services or visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Contact Information (email, phone)
  • Payment and billing details
  • ID proof (passport, license, etc.)
  • Check-in/check-out dates and room preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Confirm bookings and manage reservations
  • Send booking confirmation and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information. We may share your data with:

  • Government authorities (if legally required)
  • Payment service providers (for secure transactions)
  • Internal hotel departments (housekeeping, reception, etc.)
